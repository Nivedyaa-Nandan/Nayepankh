Who We Are

Empowering the Next Generation

Founded by Prashant Shukla, NayePankh Foundation is driven by the vision that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive. We work tirelessly in the grassroots of India to bring digital literacy, health awareness, and food security.

Have Questions?

Common FAQs

How can I trust NayePankh Foundation?
We are a registered 80G & 12A non-profit. We maintain 100% transparency with monthly audit reports available for donors.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes! We have many digital volunteering roles including social media management, content writing, and graphic design.
Are donations tax-exempt?
Yes, all donations made to NayePankh Foundation are tax-exempt under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.
